Baby Groot

Original Baby Groot sculpture created with Sculpey polymer clay following the release of Guardians of the Galaxy. He's a great companion to take around the city of Tokyo for a contrast between urban life and nature! (Character copyright Marvel)

Above: Starting with the armature, aluminum foil was used to cover the armature wire, building it to the desired form.

Below: First layer of Sculpey, building general form.

Face details adjusted to give him a more youthful look.

Backed and colored. Washes of Acrylic paint is applied, while adding details.

Time to pot Baby Groot! Foam was used to raise the layer. A mixture of ground coffee beens and glue was used for a no-mess dirt affect that hardens and stays in place.
