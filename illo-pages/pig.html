A Pig's Love |
Illustration, acrylic

Lulu took her holidays with the Altmans at their mobile home in Lake Erie. And it was here, while Jack was out fishing, that Jo Ann suffered a heart attack. "Lulu was there straight away pestering me and kissing me on the floor," recalled Jo Ann. "I thought the poor girl needed to go to her potty." Thankfully not. Lulu had realised something was terribly wrong. Jo Ann couldn't move and if the snorter didn't do something soon she was going to miss out on last night's leftovers. Fortunately, the Altmans had fitted a pig-flap and Lulu headed down to the road to stop a car and bring its owner up to the mobile home.

(Source: This is Staffordshire)
