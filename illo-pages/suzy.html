Suzy's Home

A spread from a childrens book depicting the struggles of a third culture kid. Suzy's Home follows Suzy the snail girl, and her adventure of finding her way back home after getting separated from her parents. During her journey, she meets 2 friends - Tommy the turtle boy, and Holly the hermit crab girl. With their love and support, Suzy discovers a new meaning to "home". e-book page available soon!
