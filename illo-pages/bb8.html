BB-8

BB-8 container created with Sculpey polymer clay in celebration of the release of Star Wars: The Force Awakens.

As I would for all sculptures, I start with the base of creating an armature with wires and filling up the space with aluminum foil. Once the desired shape and size is achieved, thin layers of sculpie clay are stretched and layered.

Once enough details are applied, the clay is baked to harden and painted using acrylic paint. To finish, a sealant is applied.
