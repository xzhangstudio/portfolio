Skin Stars | Illustration, digital

Freckles are like stars on the skin - there's a galaxy across the face. As an Asian growing up with freckles, I grew up being told that having freckles makes me look unkept and unattractive. Just as every culture has its cultural ideal of beauty, the Asian ideal is to have white, soft, flawless skin. As we enter a generation of a new mindset, is it not time to break these old ideals and accept our bodies that we are born with? We are all beautiful in our own way. No more self loathing, self destruction, or insecurities about being judged. Love yourself.
