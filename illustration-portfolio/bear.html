Saved By Bear

Robert Biggs had no idea that he was being stalked by a ferocious mountain lion. "He grabbed me from behind and knocked me to the ground, I was on my knees... That's when a blur on my left side grabbed the lion by its throat -- turns out it was the momma bear," he said. The cat ran away, and the bear went back down on all fours, humbly making eye contact with Biggs before regrouping with her young. (Source: The Huffington Post)

Above are initial sketches and example reference taken for this project. Usually a minimum of 2~3 sketches are made before deciding on a composition that works best. A color study was also made for this project before creating a final rough that was ready for transfer.
