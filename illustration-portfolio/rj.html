Robert Johnson

Cover depicting Robert Johnson's "Crossroad Blues" and "Hellhound on My Trail". About the artist: Robert Leroy Johnson was an influencial American blues singer-songwriter and musician. While his early career did not take off, he is rumoured to have sold his soul to the devil at a crossroad for success.

Illustration was donated to Robert Johnson Foundation.

Above are sketches/ideas of possible logos and/or illustration. Since the musician's life was so scarcely documented, the roughs maintains a theme of mystery.
