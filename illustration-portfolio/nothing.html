Nothing

The inspiration came from my thoughts on existence and what the body consists of spiritually as well as my fear of not knowing "who I am". We are able to manipulate our body physically in many ways to create a distinct identity, but I believe a true "self" comes from within. During times of existential crisis, nothing is felt, leaving only confusion and anxiety. The swirls represent this fear of nothingness (no true identity) surrounding and taking over the body.
