Zombie Taskforce

T-shirt design created for group of friends going to Halloween Horror Night at Universal Studios LA in 2013. Group was named "Zombie Taskforce" to commemorate the first inclusion of The Walking Dead at Halloween Horror Nights.

Above are initial sketches and explorations of possible logo designs that would fit the theme. All designs are centered around the idea of killing zombies.

Below are further explorations with color. Once a design is voted on and decided, further explorations are made to work out type and feel of the image.

Once happy with the design, the logo is printed on t-shirts.
