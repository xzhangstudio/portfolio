Alpacania

Mobile game design and concept for a children's game, where users can breed, dress up, and play mini games with their alpaca. Players earn coins by playing mini games and clearing mini quests, which is then used to purchase accessories and outfits for customizing the alpaca and its pen. The more love users provide for their alpaca, the higher the affection levels are raised, resulting in a higher chance of breeding rare alpaca breeds and unlocking affection specific items.
